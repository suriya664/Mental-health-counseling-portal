About Us

Our Story

Healing Minds Counseling was founded in 2010 with a simple yet powerful mission: to make quality mental health care accessible, compassionate, and effective for everyone in our community.

Our founder, Dr. Sarah Johnson, recognized a need for a practice that combined evidence-based therapeutic approaches with genuine empathy and understanding. What started as a small practice has grown into a team of dedicated professionals, each committed to helping individuals, couples, and families navigate life's challenges.

Over the years, we've helped thousands of clients find hope, healing, and renewed strength. We've witnessed incredible transformations and have been honored to be part of countless journeys toward better mental health and wellbeing.

Our Philosophy of Care

At Healing Minds Counseling, we believe that every person has inherent worth and the capacity for growth and change. We understand that seeking therapy requires courage, and we honor that courage by creating a safe, non-judgmental space where you can be authentically yourself.

We recognize that mental health challenges are complex and multifaceted. Our Therapeutic Approach

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

A structured, goal-oriented approach that helps identify and change negative thought patterns and behaviors. Effective for anxiety, depression, and many other conditions.

Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)

Combines cognitive-behavioral techniques with mindfulness practices. Particularly effective for emotion regulation, interpersonal effectiveness, and distress tolerance.

EMDR

Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing therapy helps process traumatic memories and reduce their emotional impact. Highly effective for PTSD and trauma-related conditions.

Mindfulness-Based Therapy

Incorporates meditation and mindfulness practices to help you stay present, reduce stress, and develop greater self-awareness and emotional regulation.

Psychodynamic Therapy

Explores unconscious patterns and past experiences to understand current behaviors and relationships. Helps develop deeper self-awareness and insight.

Solution-Focused Therapy

A brief, goal-oriented approach that focuses on solutions rather than problems. Helps identify strengths and build on what's already working in your life.

Diversity & Inclusion

Our Commitment

At Healing Minds Counseling, we are committed to creating an inclusive, affirming environment where everyone feels welcome, respected, and understood. We recognize that mental health experiences are shaped by diverse identities, backgrounds, and life experiences.

LGBTQ+ Affirming Care

We provide affirming, supportive care for LGBTQ+ individuals, couples, and families. Our therapists are trained in LGBTQ+ affirmative therapy and understand the unique challenges and strengths within the LGBTQ+ community.

Our team is committed to cultural competency and understands how culture, race, ethnicity, religion, and other aspects of identity impact mental health. We strive to provide culturally sensitive care that honors your background and values.
