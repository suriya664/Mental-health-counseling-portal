Individual Therapy

Individual therapy provides a safe, confidential space for you to explore your thoughts, feelings, and experiences with a trained professional. This one-on-one format allows for personalized attention and a treatment plan tailored specifically to your needs.

Who It's For

Individual therapy is beneficial for anyone experiencing:

  • Depression, anxiety, or other mood disorders
  • Trauma or PTSD
  • Stress and life transitions
  • Grief and loss
  • Relationship difficulties
  • Low self-esteem or self-confidence
  • Addiction or substance use concerns
  • Eating disorders
  • OCD or other anxiety-related conditions

What to Expect

During your first session, your therapist will conduct an assessment to understand your concerns, goals, and history. Together, you'll develop a treatment plan. Subsequent sessions typically last 50 minutes and focus on working toward your goals through evidence-based therapeutic techniques.

Duration & Frequency

Sessions are typically 50 minutes long and occur weekly or bi-weekly, depending on your needs. Treatment duration varies based on individual circumstances, but many clients see progress within 8-12 sessions.

Pricing

Individual therapy sessions: $150-$200 per session. We accept most major insurance plans and offer sliding scale fees based on income.

Couples Counseling

Couples counseling helps partners improve their relationship, resolve conflicts, and deepen their connection. Whether you're facing communication challenges, trust issues, or simply want to strengthen your bond, couples therapy can provide the tools and support you need.

Relationship Issues Addressed

  • Communication problems
  • Conflict resolution
  • Trust and infidelity
  • Intimacy and sexual concerns
  • Financial disagreements
  • Parenting differences
  • Life transitions (marriage, children, retirement)
  • Blended family challenges

Pre-Marital Counseling

We offer pre-marital counseling to help couples prepare for marriage, discuss important topics, and build a strong foundation for their future together.

What to Expect

Sessions are typically 75-90 minutes and involve both partners. Your therapist will help you identify patterns, improve communication, and develop healthier ways of relating to each other.

Pricing & Scheduling

Couples counseling sessions: $175-$225 per session. We offer flexible scheduling including evening and weekend appointments.

Family Therapy

Family therapy addresses the dynamics and relationships within a family system. It helps families improve communication, resolve conflicts, and create healthier patterns of interaction.

Family Dynamics Addressed

  • Parent-child relationships
  • Sibling conflicts
  • Blended family adjustments
  • Teen behavioral issues
  • Family transitions (divorce, remarriage, relocation)
  • Substance use affecting the family
  • Mental health concerns affecting family members
  • Communication breakdowns

What to Expect

Family therapy sessions typically include all family members or relevant members, depending on the situation. Sessions are 75-90 minutes and focus on understanding family patterns and creating positive change.

Pricing & Scheduling

Family therapy sessions: $175-$225 per session. We work with families to find scheduling that accommodates everyone.

Child & Teen Services

We provide specialized therapeutic services for children and adolescents, using age-appropriate approaches to address their unique developmental needs and challenges.

Age-Specific Approaches

  • Play Therapy (ages 3-12): Uses play as a medium for expression and healing
  • Art Therapy: Creative expression for processing emotions
  • CBT for Teens: Age-appropriate cognitive-behavioral techniques
  • Family-Involved Treatment: Parents and caregivers are integral to the process

Common Concerns

  • Anxiety and worry
  • Depression and mood changes
  • Behavioral issues
  • School-related stress
  • ADHD and attention concerns
  • Social skills and peer relationships
  • Trauma and abuse
  • Eating disorders
  • Self-harm and suicidal thoughts

Parent Involvement

We believe parents and caregivers are essential partners in their child's treatment. We provide regular updates, parent coaching, and family sessions as needed.

Pricing

Child/Teen therapy sessions: $150-$200 per session. We work with families to ensure treatment is accessible.

Online Counseling (Telehealth)

We offer secure, HIPAA-compliant telehealth services, allowing you to receive the same quality care from the comfort and convenience of your home.

Secure Video Platform

We use a secure, encrypted video platform that meets HIPAA requirements for protecting your privacy and confidentiality.

Same Quality Care

Online therapy provides the same evidence-based treatment as in-person sessions, with the added convenience of no commute and flexible scheduling.

Convenience Benefits

  • No travel time or transportation needed
  • More flexible scheduling options
  • Comfort of your own space
  • Accessible for those with mobility limitations
  • Continuity of care when traveling

Technology Requirements

You'll need a device with a camera and microphone (computer, tablet, or smartphone) and a stable internet connection. We'll provide instructions and technical support.

Insurance Coverage

Most insurance plans cover telehealth services the same as in-person sessions. Check with your insurance provider for specific coverage details.

Assessment & Testing

We provide comprehensive psychological assessments and testing services to help diagnose conditions, inform treatment planning, and provide documentation for accommodations.

Psychological Evaluations

Comprehensive assessments that evaluate cognitive functioning, emotional health, personality, and behavioral patterns. Useful for diagnostic clarification and treatment planning.

ADHD Testing

Thorough evaluation for Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder, including clinical interviews, rating scales, and cognitive testing. Provides diagnosis and recommendations for treatment and accommodations.

Learning Disability Assessments

Comprehensive evaluations for learning disabilities, including dyslexia, dyscalculia, and other learning differences. Includes recommendations for educational accommodations and interventions.

Other Assessments

  • Autism spectrum disorder evaluations
  • Personality assessments
  • Neuropsychological testing
  • Bariatric surgery evaluations
  • Fitness for duty evaluations

Process

Assessments typically involve multiple sessions, including clinical interviews, standardized testing, and review of records. A comprehensive report is provided with findings and recommendations.

Pricing

Assessment costs vary based on the type and complexity of evaluation. Most assessments range from $800-$2,500. Some insurance plans cover psychological testing.
